Privacy Policy for thnk

Last updated: January 2025

thnk is built with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data, process your voice locally when possible, and never store your audio recordings on our servers.

Information We Collect

Audio Recordings

  • Voice recordings are processed locally on your device using iOS Speech Framework
  • Audio files are never uploaded to our servers or third parties
  • Recordings are automatically deleted after local processing is complete

Text Data

  • Speech-to-text transcripts are sent to Claude AI (Anthropic) for emotional analysis
  • Analysis summaries and insights are stored locally on your device
  • No personal identifiers are included in data sent to AI services

How We Use Your Information

  • Emotional analysis: Your transcribed text is analyzed by Claude AI to provide insights and suggestions
  • Pattern recognition: Previous entries are used locally to identify emotional patterns over time
  • App improvement: We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data

Data Sharing

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Receives anonymized text transcripts for emotional analysis only
  • No advertising networks: We do not share data with advertisers
  • No data brokers: We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: All personal data is stored encrypted on your device
  • No cloud backup: Your emotional entries are not synced to iCloud
  • Secure transmission: All network requests use HTTPS encryption
  • Automatic deletion: Audio recordings are deleted immediately after processing

Your Rights

  • Delete your data: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
  • Control sharing: You can stop using the app at any time
  • Access your data: All your data is stored locally and accessible within the app

Children's Privacy

thnk is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Support: thnk Support
  • Data inquiries: Contact through the app's feedback feature
