About thnk

thnk is a minimalist iOS app designed to help you process emotions through voice recordings. Using on-device speech processing and AI-powered analysis, thnk provides insights and suggestions to help you understand your emotional patterns.

Key Features:

  • Private voice processing - Audio never leaves your device
  • AI-powered insights - Thoughtful analysis of your emotional state
  • Pattern recognition - Track emotional trends over time
  • Actionable suggestions - Concrete next steps for emotional wellness

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my voice data private?
Yes, absolutely. Your voice recordings are processed entirely on your device using iOS Speech Framework. Audio files are never uploaded to servers and are deleted immediately after processing.
What data is sent to AI services?
Only the text transcript of your voice (no audio) is sent to Claude AI for emotional analysis. No personal identifiers or device information is included.
How do I delete my data?
All your data is stored locally on your device. Simply uninstalling the app will remove all stored information. There's no cloud backup or server storage.
Why won't the app record my voice?
Make sure you've granted microphone permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone > thnk. Also check that your device isn't in silent mode if you're expecting audio feedback.
The analysis seems incomplete or strange?
AI analysis works best with clear speech and meaningful content. Try speaking clearly and sharing genuine thoughts rather than testing phrases. The more context you provide, the better the insights.
Can I use thnk offline?
Voice recording and speech-to-text work offline, but emotional analysis requires an internet connection to access Claude AI services.
